### How to read this list
Items **1–8 are non-negotiable pre-launch** (revenue, data-loss, legal, trust). Items **9–16** decide whether SaaS revenue *exists*. Items **17–25** decide whether anyone *activates and shares*. Items **26–32** are the focus/subtraction that frees a solo founder to ship the rest. Everything from 33 down compounds retention and credibility. Notably, **the majority of Tier 1–3 fixes are S/M effort** — this is a finishing problem, not a rebuild.
